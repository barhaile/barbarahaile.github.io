FAQs

Questions I hear most.

What does a Product Marketing consultant actually do?

+

I help B2B SaaS startups define clear positioning, sharpen their ICP, structure go to market strategy, and align marketing, sales, and product around one commercial narrative. From Barcelona, I work with Series A and Series B companies across Spain and EMEA that need senior product marketing expertise without hiring a full time executive.

How is this different from hiring a marketing agency?

+

Agencies optimize campaigns and channels.
I focus on positioning, messaging, ICP definition, and sales alignment. If the foundation is unclear, more traffic will not fix conversion. I fix the layer that drives revenue.

Is my Series A or Series B startup ready for Product Marketing consulting?

+

If you have traction but growth feels inconsistent, messaging is fragmented, or sales adapts the pitch on the fly, then yes. Product Marketing becomes critical when scaling exposes structural gaps.

What does a typical engagement look like?

+

Most companies start with a focused consulting project such as positioning refinement, go to market design, launch strategy, or sales enablement. After the foundation is defined, some teams retain me monthly to maintain alignment as they scale.

What is the difference between consulting and fractional Product Marketing?

+

Consulting is project based and solves a defined commercial challenge.
Fractional Product Marketing is an ongoing monthly retainer where I provide senior strategic ownership of positioning and go to market, combined with hands-on execution, without the cost and risk of a full time hire.

Can you support expansion into Spain, Italy, France, or wider EMEA markets?

+

Yes. I work in English, Spanish, Italian, and French and have built go to market strategies across Southern Europe. Expanding into Spain or France requires adapting positioning to local buyer psychology and deal dynamics, not just translating messaging.

Can you work with technical or complex B2B products?

+

Yes. Most of my experience is in fintech, SaaS, and HR tech. Complex products require sharper positioning, not more slides. I translate technical depth into commercial clarity that sales teams can use confidently.

Do you integrate AI into your consulting work?

+

Yes. I design structured workflows where AI supports research, iteration, and enablement at scale. Strategy and positioning decisions remain human led. Tools accelerate execution. Experience drives direction.
